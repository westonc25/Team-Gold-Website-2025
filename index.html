Team Gold - Smart City

A Better Way to Travel

Overview

Smart City uses technology, data, and intelligent systems to make urban life more efficient,
sustainable, and connected to help mitigate rapid urbanization. By integrating real-time data
from user and external APIs, we can reduce traffic congestion, improve public safety, and
enhance quality of life for the future of creating cities that work for the people,
not just around them.

Problem

Rapid urbanization and inadequate planning have caused severe overcrowding in cities, significantly diminishing residents' quality of life. Mass migration to urban areas, coupled with data gaps that obstruct effective urban planning, has led to strained infrastructure causing health issues, overburdened road systems resulting in traffic congestion, poor quality housing that fails to meet demand, inadequate waste management systems, water scarcity impacting daily needs, and rising crime rates threatening safety. These challenges contribute to longer commutes, unaffordable housing, and a decline in community well-being, highlighting the critical need for enhanced urban planning and resource management to address this growing crisis.

Solution

Our platform connects people and cities through real-time data and community interaction. Using outside APIs, it delivers personalized recommendations and location-based insights while offering a public forum for residents to share feedback. City partners can access a simple dashboard to view engagement trends and make data-informed decisions.

Problem Characteristics

    Congestion
    • As more people move into cities they experience congestion when traveling in their day-to-day lives.
    • If only there was a way to see how crowded areas in their communities will be and provide suggestions based on that.
    Community Interaction
    • With small community driven efforts and no platform to congregate leaves people feeling lost and out of touch with their own communities
    • If only there was a platform for the people to share data and events regarding their community in order to drive improvements
    Poor Infrastructure
    • Deteriorating infrastructure with minor investments leads to poor quality of life for the people living in these conditions.
    • If only there was a way to leverage underutilized and underfunded infrastructure to provide better living conditions for the people

Solution Characteristics

    Holistic Coverage
    • The Smart City application provides Holistic Coverage across essential sectors, specifically including health, transit, and education.
    • This broad scope helps users save time and reduce stress by allowing them to find less-crowded clinics and schools and get alternate transit routes.
    Citizen Feedback Loop
    • The solution incorporates a Citizen Feedback Loop, which is defined as a two-way process where institutions act on citizen input and report back to sustain engagement and trust.
    • This loop allows users to share and view community updates, which in turn gives city officials real-time community reports and a dashboard for better coordination and planning.
    Privacy-First Design
    • The platform adheres to a Privacy-First Design, which means the systems are built so that only the personal data necessary for a specific purpose are processed, both by design and by default.
    • Privacy is protected by using Firebase's built-in encryption both in transit and at rest via HTTPS, along with added protection through logical data isolation and access tracking.
